Pierre Gaillard

I am a researcher (CRCN) at Inria, member of the THOTH team — a joint team of Inria Grenoble – Rhône-Alpes and the Laboratoire Jean Kuntzmann.

My research focuses on statistical learning theory, with a particular emphasis on online learning: sequential prediction and aggregation of experts, bandit problems (stochastic, adversarial, sleeping, dueling), online convex optimisation, and nonparametric regression. I also work on applications to reinforcement learning and demand forecasting, and I develop the Opera R package for online prediction by expert aggregation.
